Shahi Paneer

A royal and creamy Indian cottage cheese curry

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 1 cup onions, finely chopped
  • 1 cup tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup cashews, soaked and ground to a paste
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat ghee or oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add tomato puree, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  5. Stir in the cashew paste, yogurt, and fresh cream. Mix well and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Add cubed paneer and salt. Simmer for 5-7 minutes until the paneer is well-cooked and absorbs the flavors.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.

Enjoy your Shahi Paneer!

This rich and creamy Shahi Paneer is perfect for special occasions or when you want to treat yourself to a delicious Indian dish.
