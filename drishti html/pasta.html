Spaghetti Carbonara

Simple and delicious pasta recipe

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup (about 100g) grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a skillet, cook the diced pancetta or guanciale until crispy. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and a pinch of black pepper.
  4. When the pasta is cooked, reserve a cup of pasta cooking water, then drain the pasta and add it to the skillet with the pancetta. Toss to combine.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat, and quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, tossing continuously to coat the pasta in the creamy sauce. If needed, add a bit of the reserved pasta water to achieve the desired consistency.
  6. Serve immediately with an extra sprinkle of Pecorino Romano and black pepper.

Enjoy your meal!

This Spaghetti Carbonara recipe is sure to impress your taste buds. Buon appetito!
