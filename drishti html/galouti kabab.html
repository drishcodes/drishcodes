Galouti Kabab

Delicious and flavorful kebabs that melt in your mouth

Ingredients:

  • 500g finely minced lamb or beef
  • 2 tablespoons raw papaya paste
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup roasted gram flour (besan)
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup fresh coriander, finely chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Ghee or oil for shallow frying

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the minced meat, raw papaya paste, chopped onion, ginger-garlic paste, roasted gram flour, garam masala, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, fresh coriander, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Cover the bowl and let the mixture marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight in the refrigerator.
  3. Take a portion of the mixture and shape it into flat, round patties or kebabs.
  4. Heat ghee or oil in a pan over medium heat. Shallow fry the kebabs until they are golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney or your favorite sauce.

Enjoy your Galouti Kababs!

These melt-in-your-mouth kebabs are a delight to the taste buds. Serve them as an appetizer or enjoy them with naan or paratha.
